What is this "20XX"?

This concept was founded by EskennyWorld. 20XX is the generation that represents the period after 2000. He was inspired in maintaining this concept because he is a die-hard fan of Machine Gun Kelly & wanted to represent the concept of "EST 19xx" (90s Era) that MGK represents, the game after year 2000 as "20XX" (pronounced Twenty Double X). Initially, it was a News and Gossip site about the Sri Lankan hip-hop music industry on social media such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter, but after the release of "BASNAHIRA AHASA" (බස්නාහිර අහස) in 2021, They made a record deal with Dr. BSKing and signed up with DMT Records. Now this "20XX" is an underground hip-hop group of rappers, singers, and songwriters.
